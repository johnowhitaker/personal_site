About Me

I’m a Data Scientist/AI Researcher doing R&D with answer.ai. I like teaching and making courses. My current focus is on generative AI, flitting between several modalities.

This website will hopefully become a central repository for everything I’m working on. You can already find my blog posts, videos and TILs in the ‘Everything’ feed. If you’re looking for even more info, you may also want:

  • Twitter or Sigmoid.social (@johnowhitaker on both, and most other platforms too)
  • My DataScienceCastnet YouTube channel where I document some of the projects I’ve been working on.
  • The Generative Landscape, an older (now mostly abandoned) course on all things generative.
  • My LinkedIn - although fair warning it is rarely checked or updated.
