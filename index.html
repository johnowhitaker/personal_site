About Me

I’m a Data Scientist/AI Researcher doing R&D with answer.ai. I like teaching and making courses. My current focus is on generative AI, flitting between several modalities.

This website is a central repository for everything I’m working on. You can find my blog posts, videos, TILs and just about everything else in the ‘Everything’ feed. If you’re looking for even more info, you may also want:

  • My new YouTube Channel! https://www.youtube.com/@JohnosLab where I’m trying to clear out my side-project backlog via short videos, and also secretly showing variations on a theme: simple rules leading to beautiful emergent complexity.
  • Twitter (@johnowhitaker on Sigmoid.social and bsky too, but not active there. Same handle on most other platforms as well.)
  • My DataScienceCastnet YouTube channel where I document some of the projects I’ve been working on.
  • The Generative Landscape, an older (now mostly abandoned) course on all things generative, or the even older AIAIART course.
  • My LinkedIn - although fair warning it is rarely checked or updated.
  • My GitHub
  • My book “Some Common Spiders of Zimbabwe”
  • Our book “Hands-On Generative AI with Transformers and Diffusion Models”
